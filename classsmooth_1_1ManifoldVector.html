Detailed Description

template<Manifold M, template< typename > typename Allocator = std::allocator>
class smooth::ManifoldVector< M, Allocator >

std::vector based Manifold container.

Convenient to treat a collection

\[ m = (m_1, m_2, ..., m_k) \in M \times M \times ... \times M \]

of Manifold elements as a single Manifold element \(m\).

Warning
calling size() on a ManifoldVector returns the degrees of freedom of \(M \times M \times \ldots \times M\), NOT the number of elements in the vector. For the latter, use vector_size().
